Width, Height, Padding, and Margin

Width, height, padding, and margin are on a scale of 0-12.
Just start with the first letter (w,h,p, or m) followed by a dash and 0 through 12.
To use the percentage scale, add a p after the first letter (ex: wp-3)
For padding and margin, you can add an indicator in the middle to control which sides receieve the padding or margin
Use t,b,l,or r for top, bottom, left, or right, respectively. You can also you x for left and right or y for top and bottom.
a t,b,l,or r for top, bottom, left, or right, respectively. You can also you x for left and right or y for top and bottom.
